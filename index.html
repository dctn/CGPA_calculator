What is Campify?

Campify is a campus-focused event platform designed to simplify how colleges manage events, registrations, entry validation, and certificates — without complexity.

Built for Campus Events

  • 📅 Easy event creation for fests, workshops & hackathons
  • 🎟️ Simple participant registration & verification
  • 📊 Organizer-friendly dashboard (no technical setup)
  • 🏆 Automatic e-certificates for participants

Campify is deployed only when required, ensuring cost efficiency while still delivering a reliable experience for important events.
