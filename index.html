GPA & SGPA Calculator for SRM College student (2025)

NOTE:
If you are calculating using only your current semester’s grades, you are calculating your SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) , not your CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average).
CGPA is calculated using all semesters’ grades and credits combined , representing your overall academic performance across your entire course or program.
